What gets deleted

Submitting a deletion request will permanently remove:

  • Your account credentials (Firebase Authentication)
  • All workout logs and training plans
  • Your personal profile (name, age, weight, height, goals)
  • App settings and preferences synced to the cloud
⚠ This is irreversible. Deleted data cannot be recovered. Deletion is processed within 30 days of your request.

How to request deletion

  1. Tap the button below — it opens an email to us
  2. Include the email address linked to your Tonnage account
  3. Send the email; we'll confirm receipt within 2 business days
  4. Your data will be fully deleted within 30 days

Guest mode users

If you used Tonnage in Guest Mode (no account), all data is stored locally on your device only. Simply uninstalling the App removes all data — no request needed.
